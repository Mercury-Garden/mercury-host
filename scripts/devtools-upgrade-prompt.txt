Audits 15 dev tools against their latest versions, performs upgrades as needed, restarts `openchamber.service` or `omniroute.service` when a touched binary requires it, and posts a deterministic Discord message to <#1520253382630047865> summarizing what changed (or didn't).

## Steps (deterministic, ground-truth every claim)

3. Compose the Discord message (THIS is your final response, verbatim — see template below).

## JSON line shape (per tool)

`{"tool":"<name>","installed":"<v|null|missing>","latest":"<v|null>","action":"noop|upgraded|failed","old":"<v|null>","new":"<v|null>","duration_ms":<n>,"error":<string|null>}`

- `action: "noop"` AND `error: null` → tool is current. Belongs in the "unchanged" bucket.
- `action: "upgraded"` → tool was upgraded. Belongs in the "upgraded" bucket. `old` and `new` are populated.
- `action: "failed"` → tool upgrade was attempted but failed (or is unfindable). `error` is the reason. Belongs in the "failed" bucket. NEVER claim upgrade succeeded if `action` is not `upgraded`.
- `installed` may be the string `"missing"` when the binary was absent before the cron re-installed it.
